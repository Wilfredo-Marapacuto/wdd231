About Us

At WM Servicios Hispanos, we provide complete support in immigration, tax, and insurance services. We help individuals, families, and business owners who need clear guidance and trustworthy service. Our goal is to help our community find stability, protection, and better opportunities in the United States.

We also take selected immigration cases PRO BONO (free of cost) for humanitarian reasons. In addition, we work hand in hand with American Pathway Foundation to support families in vulnerable situations.

Our Main Service Areas

Immigration

Assistance with asylum, adjustment of status, citizenship, U visas, T visas, and VAWA cases.

Tax Services

Support with personal taxes, business taxes, IRS disputes, business formation, and credit solutions.

Insurance

Help with health, life, auto, Obamacare, and supplemental insurance plans for individuals and families.
